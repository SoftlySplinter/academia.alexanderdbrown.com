About

I am a software developer for IBM, working as a tester, developer and budding test analyist for the CICS TS for z/OS in the Java and Liberty components.

In testing CICS v5.3 I was focused on Java EE 6 feature parity and z/OS connect 1.0 in CICS, particularly automated functional tests.

As a developer I am training up in the various z/OS programming skills, including HLASM, PL/X, SMP/E and problem diagnosis for z/OS. My current development experience in the CICS TS team is integrating the JCA local ECI resource adapter into the CICS Liberty JVM server.

Recently, I have been working to produce analytics on our regression suite, aiming to show the trends of test status between the various supported versions of CICS.

I graduated from Aberystwyth University with a 1st class Master of Engineering (MEng) in Software Engineering, during which time I published a paper at ISPA 2015 and was awarded the Portaltech Reply Bursary in Computer Science.

You can find more about my published work on the publications page and some of my lesser research on the essays page.
